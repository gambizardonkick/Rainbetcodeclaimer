FAQ

What is the Rainbet Code Claimer?
It's a browser userscript that automatically claims promo codes on Rainbet.com the moment they're released. It connects to our backend which monitors Telegram channels and pushes codes instantly to your browser.
Is it safe to use?
Yes. The script runs locally in your browser and uses Rainbet's official REST API to redeem codes. It doesn't access your account credentials or modify anything other than submitting promo codes.
